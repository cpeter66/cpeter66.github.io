About Our Club

Who We Are

The UNCC Photography Club is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering creativity and passion for photography among students of all skill levels.

Founded in 2010, we've grown to become one of the most active arts organizations on campus, with over 150 members.

Our Mission

To provide a supportive community where students can learn, practice, and share their love for photography through workshops, photo walks, exhibitions, and collaborative projects.
