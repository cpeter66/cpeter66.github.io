Project Design and Implementation

Project Name: 49er Shutterbugs Hub

Project Overview

Purpose: This photography club website is designed to showcase the work of UNCC Photography Club members and promote club activities. The goal is to provide a central hub for members to share their work, learn about upcoming events, and connect with other student photographers.

Intended Users: UNCC students interested in photography, current club members, faculty advisors, and the UNCC community interested in club events.

Content Overview: The website will feature a homepage with club announcements, an about page with club information, a gallery of member work, an events calendar, and a contact/join page for prospective members.

Page Design

Home

Purpose: To welcome users and introduce the photography club website. It will showcase featured member photos and upcoming events.

Audience: All visitors including prospective members, current members, and the UNCC community.

Content: Featured photos, club announcements, quick links to other sections.

Data Fields: No user input fields on this page.

Buttons: Navigation buttons to explore other pages, carousel controls for featured photos.

Actions: Clicking navigation buttons takes users to other pages; carousel buttons cycle through featured photos.

About

Purpose: Share information about the club's mission, leadership, and activities.

Audience: Visitors who want to learn more about the club.

Content: Club history, leadership bios, meeting information.

Data Fields: None.

Buttons: Links to gallery and events pages.

Actions: Navigation to other sections of the site.

Gallery

Purpose: Display photographs taken by club members.

Audience: Members and photography enthusiasts.

Content: Grid layout of images with optional captions or categories.

Data Fields: Photo submission form (front-end only).

Buttons: Filter buttons for categories, lightbox navigation.

Actions: Clicking photos enlarges them; filters change displayed images.

Events

Purpose: Showcase upcoming club activities and meetings.

Audience: Current and prospective members.

Content: Calendar view of events with details.

Data Fields: Event RSVP form (front-end only).

Buttons: Calendar navigation, RSVP submission.

Actions: Date selection shows event details; form submission shows confirmation.

Contact

Purpose: Allow users to contact the club or join.

Audience: Prospective members and general inquiries.

Content: Contact form and membership information.

Data Fields: Name, email, message, interest checkboxes.

Validation: Email format, required fields.

Buttons: Form submission button.

Actions: Form validation and submission confirmation.

Dynamic Functionality

The website will include several interactive features powered by JavaScript and jQuery:

  • Photo gallery with lightbox pop-ups and category filtering
  • Event calendar with interactive date selection
  • Form validation for contact and RSVP forms
  • Responsive navigation menu for mobile users
  • Image hover effects and transitions
  • AJAX loading for gallery images

These features will create an engaging, user-friendly experience that showcases member photography while promoting club activities.
