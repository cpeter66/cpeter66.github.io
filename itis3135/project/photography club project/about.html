About Our Club

The 49er Shutterbugs is UNCC's official student photography organization, founded in 2015. We welcome photographers of all skill levels who share a passion for visual storytelling.

Our mission is to foster creativity, share knowledge, and explore the art of photography through hands-on experiences and community engagement.
