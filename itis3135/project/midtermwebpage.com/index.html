About Me

I am an amateur photographer, and what started as just a hobby has become something I enjoy far more than I expected. I want to continue improving my skills and, eventually, turn this passion into a side job that allows me to make money doing what I love!

As I've grown up, I've come to appreciate the importance and value of photos. While many people dislike taking pictures, I believe they play a crucial role in preserving memories and capturing moments that might otherwise be forgotten.

My goal with photography is to capture moments in a way that brings back the emotions I felt at that time.
